Who? Why? How?

Delight motors is an Indian automotive pvt ltd company based out of Hyderabad, Telangana which develops the new micro car models in fully electric and hybrid models.

Delight micro car is a two seat four wheeled vehicle which is designed to combine the motorcycle and the car segment. The size, initial cost, maneuvering, mobility, running costs and maintenance will be nearly to a motorcycle. The feel and looks are adjacent to a car which protects from pollution, skin problems, climatic changes like rain, sun, cold, pot holes, slippery roads etc. Entry level hatchbacks are the only alternative next to the motorcycles in private vehicle segments yet. Delight can reduce two wheeler accidents up to 75% since motorcyclists are 30 times more likely to die than other motorists. Noval and world class design, fit & finish, standard features like infotainment, an airbag, air conditioner, abs, etc., makes this vehicle unique and feasible for all segment of people in all age groups irrespective of their gender.

Huge demands for low-cost Electric vehicles that are suited for safe short-distance urban commutes (averaging 50-100 km/trip) where Delight is exclusively a short & medium commuter. First in its segment in India as well as in international market which is more practical for all conditions in its price in automotive world. India's National Electric Mobility Mission Plan envisages to put 6 million electric vehicles on roads by 2020.
